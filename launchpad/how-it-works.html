How it Works

Step 1

Register on Magic Launchpad

Connect an EVM compatible wallet to the platform using the Binance Smart Chain Network by clicking on “Connect Wallet” at the top corner.
Select your wallet from the popup menu. We recommend using Metamask for a seamless experience.

Note:

Support for additional EVM Networks and chains is planned for the future.

Step 2

Complete KYC Verification

KYC verification is required to participate in token sales, supported by our partner SumSub.
After wallet connection, click “Get Verified” on the top right or find the KYC option in your Dashboard.
Post-verification, you will be eligible to join token sales. If you experience issues with your KYC Verification, please contact our support team.

Step 3

Understand IDO Types and Pools

Initially, all sales are first-come, first-served (FCFS). Sales close once the Raising Goal is reached.

Types of pools

Open to All

No whitelist required.

SQRp Required

Participation requires depositing SQRp Points in addition to USDT, which you can get by Staking SQR tokens or holding a Magic Store Membership.

Whitelisted Users Only

Specific prerequisites must be met for access, you can find the full details on each project page.

Step 4

Select a Project and Pool

Review the project's page to understand the available pools and requirements.
Make sure all prerequisites are completed before the sale starts, as late participation is not allowed.

Step 5

Prepare Your Wallet and SQRp Balance

Ensure your wallet contains the amount you wish to deposit in USDT (BEP20) and BNB to cover transaction fees.
If participating in a SQRp-required pool, ensure you stake enough SQRp equivalent to your USDT deposit amount (1 USDT = 1 SQRp).

Step 6

Participate in the Sale

Note the token sale's starting date and make sure to have a stable internet connection.
Visit the project deposit page at the start of the sale to deposit USDT (and SQRp if required).

Step 7

Claim Your Tokens

After the sale, a snapshot determines the eligible investors.
Wait for the Token Generation Event (TGE), after which tokens are distributed based on the vesting conditions outlined on each project page.
Tokens can be claimed via the Magic Launchpad Dashboard under "My Participations" or directly through the project, depending on the project's distribution method.
Additional details are project-specific and will be provided accordingly.

If you have any further questions Please don't hesitate to reach out to our Community Management Team. You can contact them via Telegram or Discord, and they will be happy to assist you.
